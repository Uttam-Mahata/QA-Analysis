World War 3 looms as a result of a complex web of factors, including escalating geopolitical tensions marked by nationalism, territorial disputes, and fierce competition for finite resources..
Economic frictions and trade disputes among major powers contribute to the combustible environment..
The relentless pursuit of technological dominance, particularly in realms like artificial intelligence and cyber warfare, introduces new arenas for potential conflicts..
Nuclear proliferation risks, the potential acquisition of such capabilities by non-state actors, and the destabilizing impacts of climate change and mass migration amplify the threat..
The global economy's interconnected nature, vulnerable to cyber attacks, combined with the erosion of international institutions, heightens the challenges..
Mitigating these risks demands effective diplomacy, international cooperation, and proactive conflict prevention measures to forestall the grim prospect of World War 3.
